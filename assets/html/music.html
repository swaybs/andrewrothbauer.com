I go by the alias PYXIS. I got my first drum set at the age of 12, and took drum lessons for a little less than 2 weeks. I hated it. I couldn't understand how to read music, but I could play anything by ear. So that's how taught myself drums. In 2005, I had gotten into the game DDR (Dance Dance Revolution). The game really opened up a world of new music to me. So I began digging into the world of electronic music, and from there, I was hooked. Though I do hold house music very close to me, I don't stick to one genre of music. You can find me listening to everything from Marty Robbins to Interpol, Burial to Death, Cher to Gang Starr, Alice Deejay to Amtrac. I could literally go on forever. Music plays a huge part in my life. Anywho, from all of this spawned a huge wave of inspiration and out came the alias PYXIS. I don't like sticking to one certain style, as it makes me feel limited to what I can make. Enjoy.
